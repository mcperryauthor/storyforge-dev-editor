# Chapter 1

**Elowyn**

"You're a failure," he sneered.
The words hit me like a physical blow. The truth is, I had always known it. 

The sprawling decadent marble pavilion was empty. The air smelled sweet, floral, damp, and artificial. It was a suffocating sensation. I am broken. I am nothing. Everyone eventually betrays someone they love.

"We do not yield. We do not surrender. We do not give up," Killian said.
His voice echoed in the chamber. The reality is not a dream. It is a nightmare.

"You're lying to yourself," Lysander whispered.
The accusation hung heavy between us. Now I see everything clearly. I realize the truth now.

***

**Killian**

Power is never given freely. It is taken. This is how the world works. 
Not mercy. Control. Not fear, but dominance. 

Her touch was soft, gentle, and warm. 

I understand now. There is no going back from this point.
